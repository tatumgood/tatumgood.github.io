On Ethics

Why is understanding this vignette important to use machine learning in an ethical manner?

It is important to use machine learning in an ethical manner because we need to ensure that those variables affected by our algorithmic decisions can understand the factors chosen that influenced our decisions. We make many choices throughout our work and we need to understand our impact in making those choices before we can be trusted to do so as our career.

Ignorance is no excuse

Imagine you are working on a model to predict the likelihood that an individual loses their job as the result of the changing federal policy under new presidential administrations. You have a very large dataset with many hundreds of features, but you are worried that including indicators like age, income or gender might pose some ethical problems. When you discuss these concerns with your boss, she tells you to simply drop those features from the model. Does this solve the ethical issue? Why or why not?

If the most logical point is to drop those features, then I can see the pros to moving forward with that avenue. However, I would stop and want to consider if there is any other way to anonymize the data so that we can keep the data we have. When we consider some ethical issues I feel like sometimes we can be too cautious and we try to do what is “right” but I do not want to take away from what we are trying to do with the data. In this case, this does not solve the ethical issue because it interferes with us modeling the data to get the desired results.
